About


Danae Martinez is a data science professional.
Her academic background in statistics and her experience in the business sector have allowed her to develop several technical skills in the field of artificial intelligence as well as the ability to communicate technical results to a non-specialized audience. Her programming skills combined with her mathematical knowledge allow her to design the best data science solutions to respond to the industry’s needs.
Curious and open-minded, Danae puts her expertise at the service of her clients to guide them in achieving their analytical goals and to guide them towards best practices in data governance and analytics-driven business decision making.

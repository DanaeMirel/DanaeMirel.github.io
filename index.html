About



I am a mathematician and data scientist. My interests include the application of statistical learning methods and machine learning algorithms. I’m motivated by providing value to my clients’ organisations, helping them to achieve their analytical goals and guiding them towards best practices in data governance and analytics-driven business decision making.
My academic background in statistics combined with my professional experience have allowed me to provide my clients with the best insights from data. My core skills include communicating technical results to a non-specialized audience, choosing the most appropriate statistical modeling and machine learning methods according to the situation as well as deploying solid solutions to real industry problems.
